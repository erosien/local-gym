About

The name Bhakti Basics was inspired by the phrase, "get back to basics". Our studio wanted to follow that theme by taking our students back to the core basics of what yoga truly stands for. The five basic principles of yoga are proper relaxation, proper exercise, proper breathing, proper diet, and positive thinking and meditation. We believe that by embracing these principles in every one of our classes, our students will be able to better connect with themselves and the practice on a much deeper level.
